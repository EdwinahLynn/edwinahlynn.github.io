About Us

Volunteer Connect is an organization that was founded in 1990 in Cape Town, South Africa. Volunteer Connect started small. It would only help the communities in that area due to its small resources and staff. Though it had a few and a small staff, Volunteer Connect was able to create huge and noticeable impacts.

Within five years, Volunteer Connect was now known nationally as an amazing volunteer organization and with in the next 2 years, it went international.
